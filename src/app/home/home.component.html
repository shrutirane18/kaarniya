About Us

The recent generation lacks the art of giving back to society. Our website focuses on imbibing the feeling of generosity and nobility amongst the heedless crowd. We want to create a safe place for individual to make a donation or Volunteer and create a community for all the social welfare clubs of different colleges or universities in close proximity to communicate and collaborate on different welfare projects so that society can benefit from the actions of emerging youth.
We have initiated this project to create a one-stop solution for users which includees

  • Search nearby NGO who are willing to accept donations or in need of volunteers
  • Registered NGO'S/USERS/clubs will be displayed





Search nearby

Users can search for NGO and College social welfareclub registered with our website within the vicinity where the person resides. The users can also filter the search results according to their preference of domain. (e.g., old age home, orphanage, etc).

Register as an University or College club

Our website allows various social welfare clubs from different universities to register themselves and collaborate with each other and to organize events in various non-profit organizations. Our goal is to help the process of instilling philanthropic values in youth.





Register as an individual

Karaniya allows users to register themselves as an individual and decide the cause and goal of the NGO for which they wish to donate or volunteer our website will help you get connect to NGO and provide you with the essential details for the process.
